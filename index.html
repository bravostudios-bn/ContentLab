How to Use ContentLab Tracker

Step-by-step guide for your team

1
Log In
Enter your username (bravo) and password to access the system. This ensures all credit tracking is secure and auditable.
2
Add a New Client
On the left sidebar, fill in client details and select a plan to track their content delivery.
3
Track Actual Usage (Tracker View)
Click the "📊 Tracker" button to log what you've actually delivered. Enter quantities for each content type. For "Other" content, specify the description and custom credit value.
4
Plan Monthly Delivery (Projection View)
Click the "🔮 Projection" button to plan what you'll deliver each month before client meetings.
5
Manage Settings
Click "⚙️ Settings" to update plan pricing and credits. Changes apply to new clients only.
6
Generate Monthly Invoice
In Tracker view, click "📋 Copy Invoice Text" to auto-generate a professional invoice based on actual usage.
